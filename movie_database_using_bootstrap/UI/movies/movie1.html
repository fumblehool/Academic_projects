Mukhtiar Chadha

Releasing Date – 27 Nov 2015
Star Cast – Diljit Dosanjh, Oshin Sai, Kiran Joneja, Khyali
Mukhtiar Chadha is 2015 Punjabi Romantic comedy film starring Diljit Dosanjh and Oshin Sai[3] by presentation of Eros International.[4] The film is directed by Gifty and written by Gifty while it is produced by Ohri Productions & Wahid Sandhar Showbiz with Starring Diljit Dosanjh, Oshin Sai, Yashpal Sharma, Kiran Juneja, Vikash Kumar, Jaswant Rathore and Rajasthani comedian Khayali. Dialouges of the film are written by Raman Jangwal & Manoj Sabberwal.[5] The film is scheduled to release on 27 November 2015.[2] Trailer of film was released on 22 October 2015 and was regarded as very funny.[6] Cast Diljit Dosanjh as Mukhtiar Singh Chadha Oshin Brar as Dimple Yashpal Sharma as Hussain Inderpal Singh as Mukhtiar's Father Kiran Juneja as Mukhtiar's Mother Jaswant Rathore Khayali as Hussain's gangster Vikash Kumar Abhi Rai
